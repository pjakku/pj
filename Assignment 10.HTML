2017 Indian Premier League

The 2017 season of the Indian Premier League, also known as IPL 10, was the tenth edition of the IPL, a professional Twenty20 cricket league established by the BCCI in 2007.

The tournament featured the eight teams that played in the previous season.

The new season is slated to be the biggest league tournament of its kind in the history of indian sports in terms of geographical coverage and duration.
